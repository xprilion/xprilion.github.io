Say My Name

Names - so many of them! Here's a little guide on how to pronounce mine right -

Anubhav Singh

अनुभव सिंह

/ʌ.nʊ.bʌʋ sɪŋɡʱ/

Anubhav singh
0:00
/1.4

Detailed Breakdown:

  • /ʌ/ – "a" as in cup → matches Hindi अ
  • /nʊ/ – clipped “u” as in put
  • /bʌʋ/ – "bhav" with /ʋ/, the Hindi व (labiodental approximant) — not exactly /v/ or /w/, but in between
  • /sɪŋɡʱ/ – "Singh" pronounced with:
    • /ŋ/ – "ng" as in sing
    • /ɡʱ/ – voiced aspirated "gh" like Hindi घ

Cheers!
